Admission Requirements

UNIVERSITY OF GHANA, LEGON

SCHOOL OF GRADUATE STUDIES 

GRADUATE ADMISSIONS FOR THE 2025/2026 ACADEMIC YEAR 

GENERAL ENTRY REQUIREMENTS FOR ALL PROGRAMMES

 It is announced for the information of prospective applicants and the general public that the School of Graduate Studies is offering a number of Master’s and PhD programmes for the 2024-2025 Academic Year. For the accredited programmes of the respective colleges please visit the websites of the respective schools in the relevant College as follows: College of Humanities (https://coh.ug.edu.gh); College of Education (https://coe.ug.edu.gh); College of Health Sciences (https://chs.ug.edu.gh); and College of Basic and Applied Sciences (https://cbas.ug.edu.gh).

 

PhD Programmes

Applicants may apply for a Ph.D. programme with a Masters degree or a very good first degree.

 

Applicants applying with Masters Degree

  • Applicants with relevant Masters degree from the University of Ghana or any recognised/accredited University may apply for a Ph.D. programme.

 

Applicants applying with first degree

Applicants with a very good first degree from the University of Ghana or any recognised/accredited University may apply for a PhD programme.  Such applicants, upon admission, shall be required to take first year courses of the Masters (Level 600) programme in their respective departments. They shall be required to pass all examinations in the courses.  On successful completion of the Level 600 courses and depending on their performance, they will progress to the four-year Ph.D. programme, which begins with Level 700 courses in the first year of the Ph.D. programme.

 

All Ph.D. programmes in the University are now fee-paying and with the following durations:

 

  •  Full-Time       - Four (4) years
  •  Part-Time       - Six (6) years

HOW TO APPLY

GHANAIAN APPLICANTS

University of Ghana application e-vouchers can be obtained by Ghanaian applicants for GHS220.00 at the following banks: Zenith Bank, Universal Merchant Bank, Access Bank, Ecobank, Republic Bank, Agricultural Development Bank, Consolidated Bank Ghana, GCB Bank, First Atlantic Bank and Fidelity Bank. Applicants should carefully follow the instructions on the voucher when completing the online application form.

Applicants should use the link below:

 https://apply.ug.edu.gh/admissions/graduate/login

  

INTERNATIONAL APPLICANTS AND GHANAIANS APPLYING FROM ABROAD

This category of applicants should use the following link to access the online application form for completion and submission: 

 https://apply.ug.edu.gh/admissions/igraduate/login

Account No.: 0161094409780403; Swift Code: ECOCGHAC

The pay-in-slips together with applicants’ certificates and transcripts should be scanned and attached to the application form before submission. The transcript should be scanned into one PDF document and attached to the application.

As part of the application process: 

  • Applicants are required to scan and attach copies of their bachelor’s degree certificates and transcripts in PDF format.
  • Applicants who are University of Ghana graduates must scan and attach only their bachelor’s degree certificates.

  • All applicants must provide details of three referees.

 

NOTE:
